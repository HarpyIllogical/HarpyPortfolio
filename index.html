About Me

Things about me.

I’m a dedicated Roblox developer with a passion for building immersive and engaging experiences. I started developing in 2019, inspired by a lifelong love for creating games and virtual worlds.

My skills center around 3D modeling and building, where I focus on detail and polish to bring my ideas to life. One of my proudest achievements is contributing to Noob Army Tycoon, a game that has reached over 300 million visits—a milestone that has driven my ambition to launch my own successful game in the future.

I’m committed to following through on every project I take on, with a strong work ethic and a detail-oriented approach. Although I haven’t yet worked on collaborative projects, I’m excited about the possibility of collaborating with other talented creators in the future.

Goals

  • Develop and launch my own successful game on Roblox
  • Continue refining my skills in 3D modeling and building
  • Explore opportunities to collaborate with other developers and artists
